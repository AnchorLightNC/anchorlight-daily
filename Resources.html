Recovery Resources

You don't have to face recovery alone. These organizations provide support, education, and professional help.

🆘 Crisis Support

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Call or text 988 anytime for immediate support.

🍃 Alcoholics Anonymous

Find local meetings and connect with others in recovery.

💙 Narcotics Anonymous

Support meetings for individuals recovering from drug addiction.

🧠 SMART Recovery

Evidence-based recovery meetings focused on self-empowerment.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family Support

Al-Anon and Nar-Anon offer hope and guidance for families and loved ones.

🏥 Treatment Locator

Find licensed addiction and mental health treatment providers in your area.

Asking for help is a sign of strength—not weakness. Recovery isn't about never falling. It's about getting back up one more time than you fall.
